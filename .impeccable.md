# Impeccable — design context

Persistent notes for AI and humans working on this repo. Update the **Design Context** section when product or brand direction changes.

## Design Context

### Users

**Primary audience:** **Casual players** on **web and mobile**—short (roughly **60–180 second**) levels, satisfying clears, low friction (obvious board/tray state, easy retry). Aligns with [GAME_SPEC.md](GAME_SPEC.md) on session length and casual puzzle expectations.

**Platform priority:** **Mobile first.** Touch is the main interaction; full keyboard navigation is **not** a requirement.

### Brand Personality

**Three words:** **Fun**, **relaxing**, **tactile.**

**Voice & feel:** Polished casual puzzle—clear labels, physical tiles and shadow, calm motion. Delight comes from **touch and feedback**, not from pressure or anxiety.

**Explicitly not the goal:** The experience **should not feel “addictive”** in the manipulative sense—avoid dark patterns, FOMO hooks, and overstimulation that push compulsive loops. Satisfying and repeatable is OK; exploitative is not.

### Aesthetic Direction

**Target visual language:** **Wood-first skeuomorphism**—tiles as small **beveled wood blocks** (grain, inset/outset depth), **board and tray** as a coherent “table + groove” set, **emoji** as the main color and type identity. **Palette stays small:** warm wood neutrals plus **one** accent for focus/hover/match feedback (may evolve from today’s cyan). Full phased plan, tokens, states, and asset notes: [WOOD_DESIGN_PLAN.md](WOOD_DESIGN_PLAN.md).

**Observed in [style.css](style.css) today (migration baseline):**

- **Dark theme (current default):** Navy/slate backgrounds (`--bg`, radial gradients), **cyan accent** (`--accent: #38bdf8`), cool grays for text (`--text`, `--muted`), **danger** tint for loss (`--danger`).
- **Surfaces:** Glassy top bar (blur, subtle borders), pill tray, rounded board, elevated tiles (light gradients, strong shadows)—supports the **tactile** goal and will be **retuned** toward wood/table metaphors per the plan above.
- **Typography:** `system-ui` stack—readable on small screens.
- **Motion:** Short transitions and bouncy settles; see [ANIMATIONS.md](ANIMATIONS.md).

**Light theme:** **Supported as a first-class direction**—light mode should eventually match dark in polish and clarity (not a degraded afterthought). Implementation can trail, but design decisions should work in both; wood tokens should be definable in a light variant without exploding the palette.

### Design Principles

1. **Clarity over chrome** — Coverage, tray pressure, and tappability must read at a glance on a phone; decoration must not fight the board.
2. **Calm, tactile feedback** — Motion and depth confirm taps without harsh flashing; reinforce **fun** and **relaxing**, not stress.
3. **Consistent physical language** — Board, tray, and tiles share geometry and shadow vocabulary so the stack feels like one object.
4. **Mobile-first layout** — Touch targets, safe areas, and hierarchy assume small screens first; desktop is secondary.
5. **Accessibility without a formal gate** — No mandated WCAG tier, but **strive** for solid contrast, scalable text where practical, semantics where it helps (e.g. overlays), and **`prefers-reduced-motion`** respect for animations.
6. **Respectful engagement** — Design for **fun** and return visits without **addictive** or predatory patterns.
